Short Bio

I was born in Peru and attended San Marcos University for my undergraduate studies, where I began studying economics. After earning my bachelor's degree, I worked at the Central Bank of Peru for several years. I then pursued a Master's degree in economics at ITAM in Mexico City. Fortunately, I had the opportunity to move to the United States for further education. I was accepted into a graduate program at NYU, where I completed my PhD in 2021.
